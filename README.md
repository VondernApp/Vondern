## Overview

Finding something to do shouldn’t feel like work.

Instead, you get ads, endless lists, and the same suggestions everywhere.  
Meanwhile, great places are right around the corner—you just don’t know they’re there.

## Founder Story

After traveling to nearly 50 countries, I realized something surprising:

Some of the best experiences aren’t far away at all.

They’re often in small towns, local businesses, and places people overlook every day.

But they’re hard to find.

So I built Vondern to help people discover them—and to help great places get the attention they deserve.

— Kristine Mascione  
Founder, Vondern
