Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Is Vondern Free?

+

Yes, the app is free to download and explore.

Where does Vondern work?

+

We're launching with places within about a 90-minute radius of Philadelphia. As the platform grows, new regions will be added.

How is Vondern different from Google or Yelp?

+

Vondern isn't built around reviews or popularity. It's a visual platform focused on experiences — helping you discover places you may not have thought to search for.

Do I need an account to use it?

+

No. You can browse freely. Creating an account lets you personalize your interests and save places.

Do I have to post to use Vondern?

+

No, posting is optional, but we would love for you to share so others can enjoy!

What kinds of posts are on Vondern

+

Posts focus on the experience — the place, what you did there, and why someone else should visit.

How do businesses get featured?

+

Businesses and tourism partners can join the platform. Featured placements and seasonal campaigns are available as add-ons.

FOR TOWNS AND BUSINESSES

What types of businesses are on Vondern?

+

Vondern features small towns and experience-based businesses — from restaurants and coffee shops to boutiques, breweries, farms, outdoor adventures, museums, markets, and local events. If it gives people a reason to get out and go somewhere, it belongs here. And NO CHAINS!.

Why focus on smaller towns?

+

Because they're often overlooked. Vondern helps bring attention to communities and businesses that don't always have the resources to compete for visibility.

Is this just another listing site?

+

No. Vondern is built around experience-based discovery. It's visual, interest-driven, and designed to move people from “that looks cool” to actually going.

What does a listing include?

+

Photos, a description, key details, and placement within relevant interest categories so the right audience finds you.

Do I need to manage it myself?

+

You can — or we can help. Add-on services include social support, photography, and content assistance.

How much does it cost?

+

Please check out our rates on our site.

FOR CREATORS

Who can join the Creator Directory?

+

Local creators who highlight towns, travel, food, outdoor adventures, family activities, or community experiences.

How does Vondern help creators?

+

Your content lives in a searchable directory and helps drive real-world traffic — not just saves and likes.

Do creators get paid?

+

We offer revenue-sharing opportunities and business referral incentives for creators who actively help grow the platform.
